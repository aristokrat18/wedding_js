The Couple

How did you meet?

On a balcony in college during a summer storm (it was too hot to stay inside with the power out). Megan was reading an Anne Rice novel, and Risto ate a tomato off of the plant we were growing.

How did Risto propose?

In the atrium of the National Portrait gallery, which had an exhibit on American poets. He recited some Lorca; Megan had no idea what was actually happening until he said, "I have a question to ask you."

General

Does your wedding have a theme?

We have a 1920s, lost-generation-in-Paris theme, but it's very liberally interpreted.

When is the RSVP deadline?

August 1, 2014. You can RSVP by mail or by visiting the RSVP page of our website.

Where are you registered?

After nine years together, we've got all the housewares we need, so we've set up a honeymoon registry where you can choose an activity to contribute towards.

Is there anything "traditional" that you don't plan on doing?

We won't be throwing a bouquet or tossing a garter, and we'll have pie instead of cake. We will also be forgoing the Finnish tradition of kidnapping the bride.

Travel & Accommodations

Any travel recommendations?

If you're traveling from the East coast, we recommend flying Jet Blue.

Where should we stay?

There are room blocks reserved at three nearby hotels. See the accommodations section for more information.

Guests

Are children welcome?

No, sorry. This is an adults-only weekend.

My invitation says "and guest." Do I have to bring a guest?

No, of course not. We just wanted to give you the option. Feel free to come by yourself and make friends!

What should I wear?

Cocktail attire and dancing shoes! Feel free to get into the 1920s theme with a sparkly dress or a three-piece suit if you like, but typical cocktail attire is fine too.

Food & Drink

What kind of food will be served?

There will be a cocktail hour as well as a three-course dinner and desert. We love to eat, so you won't leave hungry.

What if I have a dietary restriction?

We're happy to work with the Beehive to accommodate you—just email us beforehand to let us know the details.

Will we need to pay for drinks?

We've got you covered during the rehearsal dinner and ceremony/reception.

The Venue

Are there any additional events happening over the weekend.

Meetup at bar on Friday night, rehearsal dinner on Saturday night.

What is the venue like?

The Beehive is a unique restaurant and live music venue with a bohemian feel. Think exposed brick, velvet curtains, chandeliers, artwork, and craft cocktails. Go to the Beehive website to learn more.

How do I get to the venue?

The easiest way is to taxi from your hotel to the Beehive at 541 Tremont Street (in the South End).

Is parking available?

Yes, valet parking is available for $16.

What time should I arrive?

The ceremonty starts at 5:00 pm, so aim to get there around 4:30 to choose your seat and enjoy your welcome cocktail.

Are the ceremony and the reception all in the same place?

Yes, they are both at the Beehive.

Will there be dancing?

Oh yes. The Beehive is a live music venue, and we'll take advantage of that.

What time will the reception end?

It's officially over at 10pm, at which time the Beehive allows other patrons in as well. But feel free to stay until closing if you'd like!
